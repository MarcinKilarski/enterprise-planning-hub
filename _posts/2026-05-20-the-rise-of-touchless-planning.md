## Reaching the Next Frontier of Automation

For years, supply chain planning has been a highly manual process. Planners spend a significant portion of their workdays cleaning messy datasets, manually adjusting demand anomalies, and resolving minor inventory discrepancies in their systems. This constant manual intervention is not only slow and expensive, but it also distracts skilled planners from focusing on strategic business initiatives, vendor relationships, and long-term risk modeling.

Autonomous, touchless planning is reshaping the industry by utilizing artificial intelligence to handle routine decisions.

### Core Mechanics of Touchless Systems:
* **Machine Learning Anomaly Detection:** Identifying and correcting historical demand outliers (like promo distortions or system errors) without human review.
* **Auto-parameter Optimization:** Dynamically adjusting safety stock levels and lead times in response to real-time supply fluctuations.
* **Algorithmic Exception Routing:** Handling 90% of routine planners' tasks autonomously, only routing complex, high-impact anomalies to human experts.
