## Eliminating Wasted Trade Promotion Spend

In the Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) industry, trade promotion spend represents one of the largest items on the corporate profit and loss statement, often accounting for up to 20% of gross revenues. Despite this enormous investment, industry studies indicate that more than half of all retail promotions fail to generate a positive return. This inefficiency is driven by siloed spreadsheets, disconnected financial systems, and a lack of real-time post-event analysis.

CPG brands must shift from traditional volume-push promotional strategies to margin-focused investments.

### Key Factors for Trade Optimization:
* **Predictive Promotion Uplift Modeling:** Using machine learning to forecast the exact sales lift of specific promotions, accounting for cannibalization.
* **Integrated Supply and Demand Plans:** Aligning commercial promotional schedules directly with production planning to prevent stockouts during demand spikes.
* **Real-time Post-Event Analytics:** Evaluating promotional effectiveness immediately, enabling commercial teams to cancel or modify underperforming programs.
