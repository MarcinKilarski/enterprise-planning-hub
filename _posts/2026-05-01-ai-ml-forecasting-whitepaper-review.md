## Moving Beyond Legacy Demand Models

Traditional enterprise forecasting models depend almost entirely on trailing history. This approach struggles when managing modern omnichannel distribution networks subjected to sudden macro disruptions, supply shortages, and rapid changes in consumer behavior. Legacy models assume that the future will resemble the past, which is a dangerous assumption in today's highly volatile global markets.

### Key Takeaways from Industry Best Practices
* **Shift to Leading Indicators:** Modern environments require incorporating real-time macroeconomic datasets, social media trends, and weather patterns rather than relying solely on past sales curves.
* **Faster Scenario Optimization:** Speed-to-decision relies heavily on machine learning algorithms projecting potential supply gaps before they actually affect transit logistics.
