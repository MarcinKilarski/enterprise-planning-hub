## Modernizing Commercial Planning

Revenue Growth Management (RGM) has traditionally relied on historical sales analyses, Excel modeling, and retrospective evaluations of price elasticities. In an era defined by hyper-dynamic customer expectations, rapid channel shifts, and volatile input costs, these slow tools fall short. Enterprise leaders must digitize their pricing, promotional, and product assortment planning to capture hidden margin opportunities and outpace the competition.

### Core Focus Areas for Digital RGM:
* **Dynamic Product Assortment Optimization:** Matching localized store and regional channel demands to specific product configurations, ensuring optimal shelf space allocation.
* **Pricing and Elasticity Modeling:** Simulating the impact of price adjustments against input cost fluctuations, competitive positioning, and consumer demand curves.
* **Closed-loop Promotion Analytics:** Tracking marketing and trade spend investments against real-time sales outcomes to eliminate low-performing campaigns.
